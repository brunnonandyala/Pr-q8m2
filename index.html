What I bring to a startup

Startups move fast and ship before things are perfect — I understand that. My niche is the visual interface layer: dashboards, onboarding flows, data-heavy screens, and B2B tools where complexity has to disappear. What I do is make sure that when users arrive, the product looks like it was built by people who care. First impressions are a conversion problem. Bad UI is a trust problem. I fix both.

My design philosophy

I'm not a generalist. I'm a specialist in visual interface — the moment a user opens your product and decides whether to stay or leave. That decision happens in milliseconds. I've spent two decades engineering that moment.
